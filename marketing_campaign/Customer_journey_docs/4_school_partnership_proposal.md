## 1. Executive Summary
CodesRock is a screen-free computational thinking program designed to empower African children to speak the language of the future. We solve the two biggest hurdles schools face today: the high cost of computer labs and the growing concern over childhood screen-time. Our ecosystem provides a turnkey, tactile, and collaborative way to teach coding and robotics to students aged 4-10.

---

## 2. The CodesRock Advantage for [School Name]
*   **Zero Infrastructure Costs**: Our curriculum requires no computer lab, no iPads, and no high-speed internet. Every classroom becomes a STEM lab.
*   **Curriculum Alignment**: Our activities are mapped to develop 21st-century skills: Critical Thinking, Collaboration, Decomposition, and Algorithmic Logic.
*   **Institutional Prestige**: Schools that partner with CodesRock differentiate themselves as innovation leaders, directly addressing parent concerns about future-readiness and health.
