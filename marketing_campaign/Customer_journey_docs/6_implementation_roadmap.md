# 📅 implementation & Training Roadmap: The 4-Week Launch

This roadmap details the journey from hardware delivery to the official launch and certification of [School Name] as a CodesRock partner.

---

### Phase 1: Ecosystem Setup & Onboarding (Week 1)
*   **Activity**: Hardware Delivery & Unboxing.
*   **Goal**: Ensure all 5 Rockbots, coding cards, and curriculum guides are physically in the school and charged.
*   **CodesRock Action**: Verification of hardware functionality (The "Wiggle Test").
*   **School Action**: Designated secure storage space set aside for the ecosystem.

### Phase 2: Teacher Empowerment Workshop (Week 2)
*   **Activity**: Intensive 1-Day Training Session (6 Hours).
*   **Goal**: Move teachers from "Curious" to "Competent."
*   **Topics Covered**:
    *   The "Screen-Free" Philosophy: Why it works.
    *   Hardware Mastery: Programming the Rockbot.
    *   Curriculum Navigation: Mapping lesson plans to class time.
    *   Kinesthetic Pedagogy: Getting kids moving during "coding" time.
*   **Outcome**: Teachers receive their "Provisional Training Badge."

### Phase 3: The Integration Launch (Week 3)
*   **Activity**: First "Live" Classroom Sessions.
*   **Goal**: Introduce students to the Rockbot and the "Logic Squad" universe.
*   **CodesRock Action**: On-site mentorship. A CodesRock facilitator co-teaches the first session to provide real-time support.
*   **Student Logic Goal**: Understanding basic directional commands (Forward, Left, Right).

### Phase 4: Proficiency Review & Certification (Week 4)
*   **Activity**: Final Evaluation & Plaque Presentation.
*   **Goal**: Verify that teachers are independently leading high-impact sessions.
*   **Evaluation Criteria**: See *Teacher Proficiency Checklist*.
*   **Outcome**: 
    1.  Teachers receive their official **Proficiency Certification**.
    2.  The school is awarded the **"Certified CodesRock STEM Center"** plaque for their main entrance.
