# 🤝 Memorandum of Understanding (MoU) for Educational Partnership

**BETWEEN:**
**Codesrock Labs Ltd**, a STEAM education provider based in Ghana ("Provider")
**AND:**
**[School Name]**, located at [School Address] ("Partner School")

Collectively referred to as "The Parties."

---

### 1. Purpose of Agreement
The purpose of this MoU is to establish a strategic partnership to implement the **CodesRock Screen-Free Coding Ecosystem** within the Partner School’s curriculum to enhance students' computational thinking and 21st-century skills.

### 2. Provider Responsibilities (CodesRock)
*   **Hardware Delivery**: Provide 5 interactive robots, 5 coding card decks, and associated accessories.
*   **Curriculum & Materials**: Provide digital and physical access to lesson plans, activity books, and teacher guides.
*   **Teacher Training**: Conduct a comprehensive 1-day proficiency workshop for designated staff.
*   **Support & Maintenance**: Provide hardware technical support and software updates for a period of 3 years free of charge.
*   **Certification**: Provide official certification for trained teachers and a "Certified STEM Center" plaque for the school.

### 3. Partner School Responsibilities
*   **Investment**: Pay the initial ecosystem fee of **5,850₵** as detailed in the Formal Proposal.
*   **Integration**: Assign designated class time for the implementation of the CodesRock curriculum.
*   **Staff Allocation**: Designate at least two (2) lead teachers to undergo training and oversee the classroom sessions.
*   **Environment**: Provide a safe and clean environment for children to interact with the tactile hardware.

### 4. Intellectual Property
All curriculum materials, software, and hardware designs provided by CodesRock remain the sole property of Codesrock Labs Ltd. The Partner School is granted a non-exclusive license to use these materials for educational purposes within their institution.

### 5. Term and Termination
This agreement is valid for a period of 1 year, renewing automatically upon mutual satisfaction. Either party may terminate the partnership with 30 days' written notice.
