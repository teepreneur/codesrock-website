# 🏆 CodesRock Certification & Proficiency Pack

This pack contains the final requirements for [School Name] and its teachers to be officially certified as high-impact partners within the **CodesRock Screen-Free Ecosystem**.

---

## 1. Teacher Proficiency Checklist
*Each lead teacher must demonstrate the following skills during the Week 4 Proficiency Review:*

- [ ] **Hardware Mastery**: Can charge, turn on, and reset the Rockbot.
- [ ] **Core Sequencing**: Can successfully map a 5-step sequence using cards and have the robot execute it.
- [ ] **Debugging Demonstration**: Can explain to a student *why* a sequence failed without providing the answer directly.
- [ ] **Pedagogical Integration**: Can lead a 10-minute kinesthetic activity (the "Human Robot") where students move their bodies to represent code.
- [ ] **Portal Competency**: Can log in to the Teacher Portal and download the latest lesson plan/map.

---

## 2. Institutional Certification Criteria
*To be awarded the "Certified STEM Center" plaque, the school must meet these standards:*

- [ ] **Hardware Status**: Ecosystem is maintained in a safe, accessible storage unit.
- [ ] **Curriculum Integration**: CodesRock is formally listed on the school’s academic timetable for relevant classes.
- [ ] **Advocacy Presence**: CodesRock brochures/posters are visible in the reception or enrollment area to show parents the school's STEM focus.
- [ ] **Certified Staff**: At least two teachers have completed the individual proficiency checklist.

---

## 3. The Rewards of Certification
*Once certified, [School Name] achieves:*

1.  **The Physical Plaque**: A premium, branded plaque for the school’s main entrance:
    > "OFFICIAL CODESROCK STEM PARTNER SCHOOL: Empowering the Future, One Card at a Time."
2.  **Marketing Assets**: Access to branded photos and "CodesRock Certified" digital badges for the school's website and social media.
3.  **Advanced Access**: Priority access to Level 2 and Level 3 curricula as they are released.
4.  **Community Mention**: Inclusion in the "CodesRock Schools Directory" to help prospective parents find innovation-focused institutions.
