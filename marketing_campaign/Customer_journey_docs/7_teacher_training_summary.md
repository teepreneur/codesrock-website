# 👩‍🏫 Teacher Training Executive Summary: Empowering the Future

**Program Name:** CodesRock Educator Proficiency Program
**Target Audience:** Primary School Teachers (Early Years & Lower Primary)
**Duration:** 6 Hours (1 Day Intensive + On-site Mentorship)

---

### Core Learning Objectives
By the end of this training, teachers will be able to:
1.  **De-mystify Coding**: Understand that coding is about *logic* and *thinking*, not just syntax and screens.
2.  **Master the Rockbot**: Confidently program, troubleshoot, and maintain the interactive hardware.
3.  **Lead Guided Discovery**: Facilitate lessons using the CodesRock "Think -> Map -> Code" methodology.
4.  **Assessing Logic**: Identify when a student has understood a concept (e.g., a "Loop") through physical movement and tactile cards.

---

### Key Pedagogical Pillars

#### 1. Kinesthetic Learning (Learning by Doing)
We move away from sedentary learning. Teachers learn how to get students to "act out" the code before they program the robot. This builds deep muscle memory for logical concepts.

#### 2. The Logic Squad Universe
We use storytelling to make abstract concepts concrete. Teachers are introduced to characters like "Rocky" and "Loop-o" who help students navigate the "Planet Logix" maps.

#### 3. Error-Positive Culture (The Joy of Debugging)
In CodesRock, there are no "wrong answers," only "bugs to be fixed." We train teachers to celebrate when a robot goes the wrong way, as it creates a perfect opportunity for collaborative problem-solving.
