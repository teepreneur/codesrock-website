# CodesRock Ad Campaign: School Partners (B2B)
**Target Audience:** School Proprietors, Headmasters, Principals, and Academic Directors  
**Campaign Objective:** Lead generation for school partnerships (booking a physical product demo in their schools).  
**Key Selling Points:** Zero infrastructure cost (no computer lab needed), closing the 21st-century skill gap in KG & early grades, turnkey implementation (full teacher training & lesson plans included), and admissions competitive advantage.  
**Creative Tone:** Highly professional, prestigious, and academic. *Note: Rocky (the mascot) is excluded from these professional-touch assets to maintain a corporate aesthetic.*
