# CodesRock Teacher Portal Launch Campaign: "The Screen-Free Future of STEM"
**Launch Date:** June 30, 2026  
**Primary URL:** `codesrock.com/attendlaunch`  
**Primary Goal:** Create mass awareness of the CodesRock ecosystem, invite school proprietors & parents to the launch event, and position the Teacher Portal as a Digital Public Good (open source).

---

## 1. Launch Campaign Strategy (June 18 - June 30)

We will use the **Teacher Portal Launch** as a gateway to introduce schools and parents to the broader CodesRock ecosystem (physical robots, activity books, and mats).
*   **The Hook:** "Democratizing STEM Education." We are making the Teacher Portal **open source** so that any school can access premium preschool coding/robotics lesson plans, worksheets, and resources without expensive software fees.
*   **Top-Down (Schools):** Offer a ready-made curriculum that closes the 21st-century skill gap in KG/early grades with *zero computer lab costs*.
*   **Bottom-Up (Parents):** Empower parents to see that their children can master coding logic and robotics screen-free, making them future-ready for the AI era.
