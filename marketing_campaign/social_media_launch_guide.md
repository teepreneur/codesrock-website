# CodesRock: Facebook & TikTok Launch Guide

To expand from 12 IG followers to a global community, our new platform setups must be professional, consistent, and "Rocky-centric."

## 1. Facebook Setup (Community & Trust)
**Primary Audience:** Parents & School Teachers.

*   **Profile Picture:** Standard Rocky "Signature Pose" (branded).
*   **Cover Photo:** A collage of children playing with RockBot and Activity Books. Text: "Coding for Kids. 100% Screen-Free."
*   **Bio Template:** 
    > Helping children (ages 4-10) build computational thinking skills through screen-free play. 📵🤖 Future-proofing the next generation, one logic card at a time. 🇬🇭🚀
*   **Action Plan:**
    - [ ] Create Page.
    - [ ] Invite current WhatsApp parent contacts to Like.
    - [ ] Join "Ghana Parents" and "STEM Education Africa" groups as the Page.
    - [ ] Share "Rocky's Logic Hacks" weekly.

---

## 2. TikTok Setup (Awareness & Virality)
**Primary Audience:** Young parents & Viral discoverability.

*   **Profile Picture:** Rocky "Wave" Pose (branded).
*   **Bio Template:**
    > Coding with 0% screens and 100% logic! 📵🤖 
    > Follow Rocky on his mission to rock your brain. 🤘✨ 
    > Link in bio for Home Kits! 👇
*   **Action Plan:**
    - [ ] Create Business Account (for link in bio).
    - [ ] Use trending "Educational" or "Parenting" audio for the first 3 videos.
    - [ ] Post "The Logic Challenge" as a pinned video to drive comments.
    - [ ] Use Hashtags: #CodesRock #ScreenFree #ParentingHacks #STEM #GhanaTech

---

## 3. Universal Brand Checklist
Before posting to ANY platform, ensure:
1.  **Rocky's Rules:** (from Reference Guide) - Orange body, green belly, glowing tips.
2.  **Zero Screens:** Check background for laptops, TVs, or phones.
3.  **The Logo:** CodesRock logo in the bottom-right corner.
4.  **The CTA:** Every post must tell the user what to do (Save, Comment, Tag a Friend).

---

## 4. Growth Hacks for Small Accounts
*   **The "Tag a Teacher" Strategy:** On LinkedIn, tag 2-3 local schools or education influencers in the comments of your "Rocky Explains" posts.
*   **The "Share to Status" Loop:** Always share the new post link to your WhatsApp Status. This is where your most "warm" leads are.
*   **Engagement Hour:** Spend 15 minutes after posting replying to *every* comment with a Rocky emoji (🤘🤖).
