## 2. Voice Identity (ElevenLabs)
Rocky’s voice should be high-pitched, enthusiastic, and friendly—appealing to both kids and parents.

*   **Tool:** ElevenLabs
*   **Voice Model:** Multilingual v2
*   **Settings:**
    *   **Voice:** "Gigi" or "Freya" (Child-like/Friendly)
    *   **Pitch Shift:** +10% to +15% (to make it "mascot-like")
    *   **Stability:** 55%
    *   **Clarity/Similarity:** 75%
*   **Rocky’s Signature Catchphrase:** "Rock your brain! 🤘✨" (Use at the end of every video).

## 4. Production Workflow (The "Viral Loop")

1.  **Audio First**: Generate the Rocky voiceover in ElevenLabs using the captions from `rocky_growth_series_content.md`.
2.  **Seedance Generation**: 
    *   Upload the **Image**.
    *   Upload the **Character Reference**.
    *   Input the **Motion Prompt**.
    *   Enable **Lip-Sync** using the ElevenLabs audio.
3.  **CapCut Polish**:
    *   Import the Seedance video.
    *   Add **Auto-Captions** (TikTok users watch with sound OFF frequently).
    *   Add "CodesRock" Branding stickers.
    *   Apply a "3D Zoom" or "Night City" filter for that premium cinematic look.
4.  **Publish**: Use the hashtags provided in the content guide.
