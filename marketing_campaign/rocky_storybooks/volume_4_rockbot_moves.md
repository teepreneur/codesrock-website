## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Gear Spin!
Let’s explore gears!

1.  **The Setup:** Find a jar with a screw-on lid.
2.  **The Experiment:** 
    *   Try to twist the lid off by holding it close to the center (hard).
    *   Now, hold the lid by the outer edge and twist (easier).
3.  **The Logic:** The larger circle gives you more leverage (turning force), just like a larger gear!
4.  **Mat Challenge:** Place **RockBot** on a ramp on your mat. Program it to move up. In your mind, thank the gears inside RockBot for giving it the strength to climb!

## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Ruler Lever!
Let’s build a mini lever!

1.  **The Setup:** Find a ruler (the beam) and a marker/crayon (the fulcrum). Place the ruler across the marker.
2.  **The Load:** Place a heavy coin or small toy on one end of the ruler.
3.  **The Test:** 
    *   Put the marker in the middle. Push the empty end of the ruler to lift the toy.
    *   Now, slide the marker close to the toy. Push the empty end again.
4.  **The Observation:** Which way was easier to lift the toy? When the marker was closer to the toy! You just engineered a lever!
