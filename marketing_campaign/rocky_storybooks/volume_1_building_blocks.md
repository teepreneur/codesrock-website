## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Bell-Ringer Game!
Let’s use our CodesRock cards and mat to run our own event-driven program!

1.  **The Setup:** Place your **CodesRock Grid Mat** on the floor. Put a toy (the "Drum") at one end of the mat, and **RockBot** at the other end.
2.  **The Event:** Designate one person (or a toy bell) to be the "Event Trigger." When they shout **"BONG!"**, the Programmer must place a green **"START"** card.
3.  **The Code:** Build a path of cards to guide RockBot to the drum. 
4.  **The Rule:** RockBot cannot move until the Event ("BONG!") happens! Once the event fires, run your code and dance when RockBot reaches the drum!

## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The River Crossing!
Let’s use our CodesRock cards and mat to build a conditional crossing!
