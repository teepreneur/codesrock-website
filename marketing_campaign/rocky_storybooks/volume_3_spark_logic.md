## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Human Circuit!
Let’s build a human circuit!

1.  **The Setup:** Stand in a circle with a parent or friend. Hold hands.
2.  **The Rule:** When everyone is holding hands, the circuit is **CLOSED** (connected). When you let go of one hand, the circuit is **OPEN** (broken).
3.  **The Flow:** 
    *   Start with hands held. One person squeezes their neighbor’s hand. 
    *   Squeeze hands in a circle. When the squeeze returns to the start, shout **"LIGHT ON!"**
    *   Now, let go of one hand. Try to send the squeeze. You can’t, because the circuit is open!
4.  **Connect:** Hold hands again and complete the circle! You just ran a physical circuit!

## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Gatekeeper Game!
Let’s play a logic gate game on the mat!

1.  **The Setup:** Put two objects at the end of the mat (e.g. a red block and a blue block). Put **RockBot** at the start.
2.  **The AND Gate Rule:** 
    *   To cross the finish line, RockBot must be holding the red block **AND** the blue block.
    *   Program RockBot to roll, pick up both, and cross!
3.  **The OR Gate Rule:** 
    *   To cross, RockBot only needs the red block **OR** the blue block.
    *   Program RockBot to pick up whichever block is closer and cross!
4.  **Compare:** Which game was faster? The OR gate game, because it only needed one condition!
