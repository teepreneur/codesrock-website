## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Connected Network!
Let’s build a human neural network!

1.  **The Setup:** Stand in a line with 3 players (Player 1, Player 2, Player 3). Put a basket of toys at the start.
2.  **The Target:** You want to find only the **Blue Blocks**.
3.  **The Network Roles:**
    *   **Player 1 (The Shape Node):** Picks up a toy. IF it is a block, they pass it to Player 2. (If it is a ball, they put it back).
    *   **Player 2 (The Color Node):** Takes the block. IF it is blue, they pass it to Player 3. (If it is red, they put it back).
    *   **Player 3 (The Output Node):** Places the blue block in the target basket!
4.  **Run the Code:** Run the network as fast as you can. You just built a physical neural network!

## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Practice Bowl!
Let’s practice machine learning!

1.  **The Setup:** Place a bowl or cup on the floor. Stand 3 steps away. Collect 5 paper balls (screwed-up paper).
2.  **The Training:** 
    *   Close your eyes and throw the first paper ball. Did it hit? (Probably not!).

3.  **The Goal:** Keep adjusting until you hit the bowl 3 times in a row!
4.  **Logical Lesson:** Your brain just ran a machine learning loop, adjusting its motor control based on error feedback!

## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Mango Harvest!
Let’s play a computer vision game on the mat!

1.  **The Setup:** Place several toys of different colors (e.g. red, yellow, blue) on your **Grid Mat**.
2.  **Define the Ripe Feature:** Decide that **Yellow** is "Ripe" and **Red/Blue** are "Unripe."
3.  **The Game:** Move RockBot across the mat. 
    *   At each square, look through a cardboard tube (your "camera lens").
    *   IF you see a yellow toy, pick it up and place it in the basket!
    *   IF you see a red or blue toy, roll past it!
4.  **Logical Lesson:** You just programmed a computer vision path! How many ripe mangoes did you collect?
