## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Magic Dance Recipe!
Let’s create a dance function!

1.  **The Setup:** Grab a yellow card (or a piece of yellow paper) and write **"MY DANCE"** at the top. This is your Function card!
2.  **The Recipe:** Under the title, place 3 action cards: **"FORWARD"**, **"CLAP"**, **"TURN RIGHT"**.
3.  **The Call:** When the parent shouts **"MY DANCE!"**, the child (acting as RockBot) must perform all three steps in order.
4.  **Repeat:** Try calling the function 3 times. See how easy it is to repeat the whole dance with just one shout!

## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Index Train!
Let’s build a physical array!

1.  **The Setup:** Find 4 cups or small boxes. Line them up. Label them: **0, 1, 2, 3**. This is your Array!
2.  **The Items:** Grab 4 different toys (e.g. a car, a block, a crayon, a coin).
3.  **Load the Array:** Place one toy in each cup. Remember which toy is in which cup!
4.  **Query the Array:** Have a parent call out an index: **"What is in index 1?"** or **"What is in index 3?"**. The child must pull the correct toy from that cup. Try it back and forth!

## 🛠️ Unplugged Quest: The Superpower Family Tree!
Let’s practice inheritance!

1.  **The Setup:** Grab a big toy (the "Parent") and a small toy (the "Child").
2.  **Define Parent Skills:** Write two skills on slips of paper and place them under the Parent toy (e.g. **"ROLL"** and **"TALK"**).
3.  **The Inheritance Rule:** The Child toy automatically gets those two skills. Move the Child toy around and make it "roll" and "talk" using the parent's slips of paper.
4.  **Add a Unique Skill:** Write a new skill (e.g. **"FLY"**) and place it *only* under the Child toy. Now the Child can Roll, Talk, AND Fly! The Parent can still only Roll and Talk. You just created a subclass!
