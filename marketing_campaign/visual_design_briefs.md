# CodesRock Visual Design Briefs
**Brand Identity:** Innovative, Playful, Premium, African-Centric.
**Constraint:** All assets must feel "Screen-Free" (focus on hands, robots, physical cards, smiling faces).

---

## 1. Brand Guidelines (Strict Adherence)

### Color Palette
*   **Primary Blue (Trust/Tech):** `#46C5D5` (Dominant background/overlay color)
*   **Action Orange (CTA/Energy):** `#FF7340` (Buttons, key highlights)
*   **Creative Pink:** `#EC4899` (Accents)
*   **Growth Yellow:** `#FDC82F` (Badges, stars)
*   **Deep Purple:** `#5D3B98` (Text, strong headers)

### Typography
*   **Headlines:** *Nunito* (Rounding, friendly, bold).
*   **Body Text:** *Inter* (Clean, legible).

### Imagery Style
*   **Subject:** Diverse African children (ages 4-10) in bright, modern home or classroom settings.
*   **Action:** Kids looking at *each other* or the *robot*, NOT at screens. Hands-on interaction.
*   **Lighting:** High-key, natural light, vibrant. Avoid dark/moody tones.

---

## 2. Asset Request List

### A. The "Hero" Social Image (Facebook/IG)
*   **Scene:** Close up of a diverse group of 3 kids (African decent) on a colorful rug. In the center is the CodesRock robot (white/orange friendly robot).
*   **Action:** One child is placing a coding card on the floor, others are cheering/pointing.
*   **Overlay:** **Mandatory:** Use the provided `CodesRock` wide logo (multicolor text with monsters) in the bottom right corner. Do not generate generic text.

### B. The "School Partner" Flyer (LinkedIn/Print)
*   **Layout:** Split screen.
*   **Left Side:** Photo of a teacher standing confidently next to a stack of CodesRock kits in a classroom.
*   **Right Side:** Clean white background with Bullet points (use Checkmarks in `#46C5D5`).
    *   No Computer Lab Needed
    *   Teacher Training Included
    *   Curriculum Aligned
*   **Branding:** Use the "CR" Square Logo (Dark background) as a badge in the top corner.

### C. The "Product Showcase" (Website/Ads)
*   **Subject:** Top-down view (Knolling style) of the Home Kit contents.
*   **Items:** The Robot, the brightly colored Activity Book (Featuring the Wide Logo), a spread of Coding Cards, the Map.
*   **Background:** Clean `#46C5D5` (Cyan) background or light wooden table.
*   **Vibe:** Organized, premium, "gift-ready".

---

## 3. Video Direction (TikTok/Reels)
*   **Pacing:** Fast. 0.5s cuts for the first 3 seconds.
*   **Music:** Afro-pop instrumental or Upbeat Tech background.
*   **Key Shot:** The "Aha!" moment. Zoom in on a child's face when the robot successfully completes the maze.

---

## 4. The Official Mascot: Rocky "The Logic Star"

### Character Profile
*   **Name:** Rocky
*   **Origin:** Planet Logix
*   **Species:** Logic Star Alien (Fusion of the logo's Orange and Green monsters).
*   **Core Mission:** To show kids that "Coding is the rhythm of the future."

### Visual Design Rules
*   **Anatomy:** Vibrant orange body with lime-green accent patterns. Two bulbous antennae that glow when thinking or celebrating.
*   **Texture:** High-quality 3D "soft-touch" finish (Pixar-lite).
*   **Expressions:** Always high-energy, curious, or encouraging. Never frustrated or angry.
*   **Colors:** Primarily uses `#FF7340` (Orange) and `#46C5D5` (Blue backdrop).

### 2D Vector Variant (For Social/Print)
*   **Aesthetic:** Flat 2D vector illustration with a refined tactile touch. 
*   **Character Texture:** **Heritage Dots** (subtle darker-orange dot pattern on the body, inspired by the original logo monsters) is required to add depth to the 2D form.
*   **Narrative Background:** Prioritize the **Planet Logix** environment for introductory and high-impact posts. This includes vibrant purple/cyan skies, floating logic-block islands, and bioluminescent flora. Subtly integrate coding symbols and gears into the alien nature and architecture.
*   **Secondary Background:** For quick slides or infographics, use the **Logic Pattern** (minimalist white icons on brand blue).
*   **Usage:** Best for infographics, quick social media slides, flyers, and merchandise (stickers/t-shirts).



### Narrative Voice & Tone (Social Media Bot/Narrator)

*   **Perspective:** Rocky speaks in the first person ("I," "My").
*   **Visual Narrative Style:** **MANDATORY: Logic Spark Cloud**. Rocky always speaks via a high-end, semi-transparent 3D glassmorphic speech bubble floating near him. This makes him feel like an interactive social bot.
*   **Key Traits:** Enthusiastic, rhythmic, pun-loving (especially music/coding puns).

*   **Signature Sign-off:** "Keep Rocking the Code! 🤘" or "Logic sparks flying! ✨"
*   **Engagement Style:** Ask interactive questions. "Which coding card would YOU use here?" "Can you hear the logic beat?"
*   **Constraint:** Rocky NEVER mentions screens. Focus is always on the Physical Robot, the Cards, and the Map.
