# CodesRock Recruitment Campaign: Interns & Volunteers
**Target Audience:** High School (SHS) Leavers, University Students, and early-career Education/Tech enthusiasts in Ghana.  
**Role:** Content Development & Video Production Assistant (Intern/Volunteer)  
**Objective:** Recruit hands-on talent to help develop lesson resources and film instructional videos for the CodesRock Teacher Portal launch.  
**Key Selling Points:** Real-world experience in EdTech, video production and editing portfolio building, networking with UNICEF-backed startup leaders, and hands-on training in coding & robotics.
