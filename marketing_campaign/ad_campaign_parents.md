# CodesRock Ad Campaign: Parent Ambassadors
**Target Audience:** Parents of children aged 3–8 (Preschool, KG, and early grades)  
**Campaign Objective:** Lead generation for the Ambassador Program (parents who advocate for CodesRock to be adopted by their child's school) and direct home kit sales.  
**Key Selling Points:** Screen-free learning, future-proofing for the AI/robotics era, academic mastery, tactile play.

---

## 1. Facebook & Instagram Ad Set 1: "The AI Dilemma" (Problem-Solution)
*   **Ad Format:** Single Image / Carousel
*   **Ad Concept:** A parent wants their child to be future-ready for the AI age, but hates seeing them glued to a screen. CodesRock is the bridge.
*   **Mascot Voice:** High (Rocky acts as the friendly guide explaining how code works).

### Ad Copy:
**Headline:** Future-proof their mind. Without the screen time. 📵🤖  

**Primary Text:**  
"Every parent is asking: *How do I prepare my child for the future of AI and robotics without destroying their eyesight on a tablet?* 📱👁️

With **CodesRock**, they learn the exact same coding concepts—algorithms, loops, and variables—using physical, tactile robots and logic cards. No screens. Just pure hands-on intelligence. 

Give your preschooler or primary schooler the head start they deserve, right from your living room.

👉 Click **Sign Up** to order your Home Starter Kit and join our parent community today!

#CodesRock #ScreenFreeCoding #FutureReady #ParentingHacks #STEMKids"

### Visual Creative Brief:
*   **Style:** 3D Pixar-lite cinematic render.
*   **Scene:** A cozy, warm-lit living room. A 5-year-old child is sitting on a colorful play mat on the floor, happily placing green arrows and orange logic cards in front of a friendly white-and-orange robot (RockBot). Next to the child, **Rocky** (the friendly orange alien mascot with thick green-tipped antennae) is sitting on the floor pointing at the robot with a big, proud smile. No phones, TVs, or screens are in sight.
*   **Overlay Text:** "Coding & AI Mastery. 100% Screen-Free."
