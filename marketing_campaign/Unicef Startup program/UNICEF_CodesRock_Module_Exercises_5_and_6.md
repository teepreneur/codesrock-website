### Exercise 6: Value Fulfillment Blueprint (VFB)
*This blueprint demonstrates how we deliver value to our end-users (Children) by leveraging our true customers/advocates (Parents) to reach our primary buyers (Schools).*

#### 1. The Customer Problem (The "Job to be Done")
*   **Parents (Akosua):** Need a way to future-proof their children with computational thinking skills without the negative physical/social effects of increased screen time.
*   **Schools:** Need to modernize their curriculum with STEM to stay competitive and please parents, but they lack the budget for computer labs and the technical teachers to run them.

#### 2. The CodesRock Solution
A completely screen-free coding curriculum utilizing physical Activity Books, Logic Flashcards, Original Songs, Unplugged physical games, and an interactive Robot (Rockbot).

#### 3. The Value Fulfillment Pathway (How Value is Delivered)
*   **Step 1: Grassroots Demand Generation.** We target parents (Akosua) with the "Screen-Free" marketing message. We convert her anxiety around screen-time into advocacy.
*   **Step 2: The Introduction.** Akosua acts as our internal champion, introducing CodesRock to the School Administration. *Insight: A warm introduction from a paying parent holds 10x more weight than a cold B2B sales email.*
*   **Step 3: The B2B Pitch.** The CodesRock team presents the solution to the School Head. We highlight that we solve *both* Akosua's problem (screen-free STEM) and the School's problem (no computer lab needed, teacher portal provided).
*   **Step 4: Curriculum Integration.** The school purchases the CodesRock ecosystem for the entire classroom.
*   **Step 5: Impact Delivery.** The child (end-user) now receives consistent, collaborative, screen-free coding education built into their academic routine, fulfilling Akosua's original need at scale.

#### 4. Key Takeaway & Business Model Insight
**Parents are our primary advocates, but schools are the optimal integration point.** While a direct-to-consumer (Home Kit) model exists, the ultimate value fulfillment occurs when CodesRock is woven into a school's curriculum. This model transforms a single parent's need into an institutional contract that benefits all children in that environment.
