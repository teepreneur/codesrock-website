## 💼 LinkedIn
*Focus: Professional, impact-oriented, and strategic.*

**Caption:**
We are thrilled to announce that **Codesrock Labs Ltd** has been officially selected for the **UNICEF StartUp Lab Cohort 6.** 🤝🌍

This selection—supported by UNICEF and partners like KOICA—validates our core mission: bridging the early childhood STEM gap across Africa through a "lab-less" approach. 

With 65% of children entering primary school today destined for jobs that don't yet exist, the ability to think computationally is more than a skill—it’s a necessity. However, high infrastructure costs and screen-time concerns often keep these tools out of reach.

At Codesrock, we solve this with our tactile ecosystem. By using logic cards and our interactive mascot, Rocky, children master the fundamentals of algorithms and problem-solving through kinesthetic play—zero computer lab required. 🤖📵

We look forward to leveraging this program to scale our impact and ensure that the next generation of African innovators is ready for the future. 🚀
