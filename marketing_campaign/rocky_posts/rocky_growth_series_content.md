## Series 1: Rocky's Unplugged Logic Hacks (3 Posts)
**Goal:** High Saves & Shares (Algorithm growth).

### Post 1.1: The Sock Sort Algorithm
**Visual Brief:** Rocky sitting on a laundry basket full of colorful socks. He is holding two matching socks with a look of triumph. 
**Speech Bubble:** "Sorting laundry? No, I'm running a matching algorithm! 🧦✨"
**Caption:** 
Did you know your laundry basket is a coding playground? 🧦🧠

When your child matches socks, they are practicing **Pattern Recognition**—a core foundation of coding! 

**The Hack:** Ask them to find all the "blue" socks first (Filtering), then match the pairs (Mapping). You just ran a search algorithm without a single screen! 📵🤘

Save this for your next laundry day! 📌

## Series 3: Rocky Explains (3 Posts)
**Goal:** High Authority (LinkedIn growth).

### Post 3.1: What is an Algorithm, anyway?
**Visual Brief:** Rocky in a "Professor" persona, wearing holographic glasses, pointing at a glowing "Algorithm" flowchart floating in the air. 
**Speech Bubble:** "It's not magic, it's just a recipe for success! 📋✨"
**Caption:**
"Algorithm" sounds like a big, scary word. But to a child, it’s just a recipe. 🍳🧠

At CodesRock, we teach kids that an algorithm is simply a sequence of steps to complete a task. Whether it's brushing their teeth or programming RockBot to navigate a maze, the logic is the same.

By mastering the *concept* of algorithms screen-free, they build a mental framework that makes learning Python or C++ a breeze later on. 🚀

Education is changing. Are your students ready? 🎓🤘
