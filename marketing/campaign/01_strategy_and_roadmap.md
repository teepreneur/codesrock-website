# CodesRock: Marketing Strategy & Roadmap (Q2 2026)

## 🎯 Campaign Objectives
1. **B2B (Schools):** Secure 50 new partner schools for the 2026 academic year.
2. **B2C (Parents):** Sell 500 Home Starter Kits.
3. **Brand Awareness:** Position CodesRock as Ghana’s premium, research-backed solution for early childhood computational thinking.

## 👥 Target Audience
### 1. School Representatives & Principals
- **Pain Points:** Need to modernize curriculum, stand out from competitors, lacking trained IT teachers for early years.
- **Value Proposition:** Turnkey STEM solution (curriculum + hardware + training). Opportunity for extra school revenue. Differentiation.
- **Key Channels:** LinkedIn, Direct Email, Headteacher Associations.

### 2. Parents (Ages 25-45)
- **Pain Points:** High screen time concerns, wanting to give kids a competitive edge, looking for educational but fun offline activities.
- **Value Proposition:** Screen-free, builds brain power, independent play, future-proof logic skills.
- **Key Channels:** Instagram, Facebook Groups, WhatsApp Status, Influencer partnerships.

---

## 🗺️ 3-Month Marketing Roadmap

### Month 1: Awareness & Trust Building
- **Theme:** "The Screen-Free Advantage"
- **Actions:**
  - Launch organic social media content showing the dangers of too much screen time vs. the CodesRock approach.
  - Announce the "2026 School Partnership Program".
  - Send introductory cold emails to top 200 preschools in Accra/Kumasi/Tema.
- **Goal:** Reach 50,000 accounts on social media; book 15 school demos.

### Month 2: Product Education & Engagement
- **Theme:** "Play. Think. Code. Grow."
- **Actions:**
  - Deep-dive into the Kit components (Robot, Activity Books, Flashcards, Songs).
  - Launch paid Meta Ads targeting parents with the "Home Starter Kit".
  - Host a free webinar/demo for school heads: "Integrating STEM in Early Years without Screens".
- **Goal:** 200 Home Kit sales; book 25 school demos.

### Month 3: Social Proof & Conversion
- **Theme:** "Real Results from Ghana"
- **Actions:**
  - Share testimonials from current school partners (e.g., Little Explorers, GIS) and parents.
  - Publish the "+200% problem-solving improvement" impact report.
  - "Last chance" scarcity push for 2026 school onboarding.
- **Goal:** 300 Home Kit sales; sign 50 school contracts total.

## 🎨 Brand & Design Guidelines (From Website)
All marketing assets must strictly adhere to this design language.

### Typography
- **Headings:** Nunito (Friendly, rounded, approachable)
- **Body:** Inter (Clean, highly legible)

### Core Color Palette
- **Brand Blue:** `#46C5D5` (Primary action, trust, technology)
- **Brand Orange:** `#FF7340` (Excitement, Home Kit, CTA buttons)
- **Brand Pink:** `#EC4899` (Playfulness)
- **Brand Yellow:** `#FDC82F` (Creativity, Parent focus)
- **Brand Purple:** `#5D3B98` (Impact, premium feel)
- **Brand Red:** `#CE3845` (Robot/Core kit color)

### Visual Style
- **Backgrounds:** Off-white (`#FAFAFA`) with subtle colorful blurred blobs.
- **Elements:** Glassmorphism panels, soft rounded corners (`rounded-[2rem]`), drop shadows.
- **Vibe:** Joyful, premium, educational, Ghanaian context, vibrant.
- **Logo Usage:** Always use the full logo or the icon prominently, ensuring it maintains its proportions. Do not distort or recolor.
